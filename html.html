Student Clubs at VIT Pune

Explore Your Passions, Build Your Skills

Welcome to the Hub of Innovation and Creativity

At VIT Pune, student clubs are an integral part of the campus experience. They provide a platform for students to explore their interests beyond academics, develop new skills, and connect with like-minded peers. From technical societies to cultural groups, there's a place for everyone to thrive.

Featured Clubs

V-Enthuse (E-Cell)

The Entrepreneurship Cell of VIT Pune, fostering innovation and a startup culture. V-Enthuse organizes workshops, competitions, and speaker sessions to inspire the next generation of entrepreneurs.

Mirage - The Cultural Club

Mirage is the official cultural club of VIT Pune. It's the heart of all cultural activities on campus, managing dance, drama, music, and fine arts events, including the annual cultural fest, Vishwakarandak.

Art Circle

A creative space for artists to express themselves. The Art Circle conducts workshops, exhibitions, and collaborative projects, promoting visual arts and creativity across the campus.

IEEE Student Branch

The IEEE Student Branch at VIT Pune is a technical community dedicated to advancing technology. It organizes seminars, technical workshops, and coding competitions to enhance students' technical knowledge.

Robotics Forum

For students passionate about robotics and automation. The forum works on innovative projects, participates in national competitions, and helps students gain hands-on experience in the field.

Sports Club

Promoting a culture of fitness and sportsmanship. The Sports Club organizes various intra-college and inter-college tournaments for cricket, football, basketball, and many other sports.

How to Join a Club

Getting involved is easy! Most clubs conduct a recruitment drive at the beginning of the academic year.

1. Explore

Attend the club orientation sessions to learn about different clubs and find the ones that match your interests.

2. Apply

Follow the clubs on their social media handles and look out for recruitment announcements. Fill out the application forms for the clubs you wish to join.

3. Participate

Engage in the selection process, which may include interviews or auditions, and become a part of the vibrant club culture at VIT Pune.
